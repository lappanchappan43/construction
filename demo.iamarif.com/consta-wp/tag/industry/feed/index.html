Building Radar is all about reducing pain and collecting data. This is why we wanted to make some construction data for the European markets freely available here. This site gives an overview of statistical data from a vast amount of sources. Due to missing figures in some countries, it was not possible up until to date to provide a full set of information on every country. This market data is the most recent available, which for the most part has been raised between 2012 and 2014. No set is older than 2011. The major parts of the construction industry in Europe publicized data for 2014. This will thus be the base for the following data.
